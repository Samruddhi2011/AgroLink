Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AgroLink

How can we help you?

Account & Registration

How do I create an account on AgroLink?
To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. Choose your account type (Farmer, Buyer, or Expert), fill in your details, verify your mobile number or email, and you're ready to go! The sign-up process takes less than 5 minutes.
What's the difference between farmer, buyer, and expert accounts?
  • Farmer accounts can list crops for sale, access weather updates, seek expert advice, and participate in community forums.
  • Buyer accounts can browse and purchase crops directly from farmers, set up buying preferences, and connect with farmers.
  • Expert accounts are verified agricultural specialists who can provide advice, publish articles, and interact with farmers on the platform.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only share your contact details with other users when necessary for transactions, and you can control your privacy settings from your account dashboard.

Marketplace

How do I list my crops for sale?
From your Farmer Dashboard, click on "List New Crop." Fill in details about your crop including type, quantity, quality, expected harvest date, and your asking price. You can also add photos of your crops. Once submitted, your listing will be visible to potential buyers.
How are payments handled?
AgroLink offers multiple payment options: direct bank transfers, UPI, and our secure AgriPay system. Once a buyer places an order, they can choose their preferred payment method. Funds are held in escrow until delivery is confirmed, ensuring security for both parties.
What fees does AgroLink charge?
Basic accounts are free. AgroLink charges a 2% transaction fee on successful sales. Premium accounts have additional features at ₹299/month for farmers and ₹499/month for buyers, with reduced transaction fees of 1%.

Weather & Advisory Services

How accurate are the weather forecasts?
Our weather data comes from multiple meteorological sources including IMD (Indian Meteorological Department) and satellite data. The forecasts are typically accurate with a confidence level of 85-90% for 3-day forecasts and 70-75% for 7-day forecasts.
How do I get personalized crop advice?
Navigate to the "Expert Advice" section and select your crop type and specific issue. You can post questions that will be answered by verified agricultural experts within 24 hours. Premium members get priority responses and can schedule video consultations with experts.
Can I set up weather alerts for my farm location?
Yes! Go to the Weather page and click "Set Up Alerts." You can set your exact farm location using GPS coordinates or our map interface, then choose which types of alerts you want to receive (rain, temperature extremes, frost warnings, etc.) and how you want to receive them (SMS, email, app notification).

Community & Support

How do I participate in the community forum?
The Community Forum is accessible from the main menu. You can browse existing discussions by topic, create new threads, or respond to other users' questions. Forums are moderated to ensure respectful and helpful interactions.
What should I do if I encounter a problem?
For technical issues, please click on "Help" in the bottom corner of any page or email support@agrolink.com. Our support team is available 7 days a week from 8 AM to 8 PM. For urgent matters, you can call our helpline at 1800-123-4567.
How can I get training on using AgroLink?
We offer free training sessions every Wednesday at 4 PM via our mobile app. You can also access tutorial videos in the "Learning Center" section. For in-person training, check if we have workshops scheduled in your district on our Events page.
