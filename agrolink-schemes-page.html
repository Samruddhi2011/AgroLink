PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
Subsidy Active Last Updated: March 15, 2025
Apply Now

An income support scheme providing up to ₹6,000 per year to farmer families in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each. The scheme covers small and marginal farmers with landholdings up to 2 hectares.

Benefits:
  • Direct income support of ₹6,000 annually
  • Money transferred directly to bank accounts
  • No intermediaries involved
Documents Required:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Records
  • Bank Account Details
Eligibility:
  • Small and marginal farmers
  • Land holding up to 2 hectares
  • All states and UTs covered

PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)
Insurance Active Last Updated: February 20, 2025
Apply Now

A crop insurance scheme providing financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events. It aims to stabilize the income of farmers to ensure their continuance in farming.

Benefits:
  • Comprehensive risk coverage
  • Low premium rates
  • Full crop cycle coverage
Documents Required:
  • Land Records / Tenancy Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
Eligibility:
  • All farmers growing notified crops
  • Both loanee and non-loanee farmers
  • Tenant farmers with proper documentation

KCC (Kisan Credit Card)
Loan Active Last Updated: January 5, 2025
Apply Now

The Kisan Credit Card scheme provides farmers with affordable credit for their agricultural needs. The scheme covers financial requirements for agriculture, allied activities, and non-farm activities.

Benefits:
  • Flexible credit limits
  • Low interest rates (4% with interest subvention)
  • Personal accident insurance coverage
Documents Required:
  • Land ownership records
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN)
  • Passport size photographs
Eligibility:
  • Individual farmers
  • Joint borrowers
  • Tenant farmers with proper documentation

NMSA (National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture)
Subsidy Education Active Last Updated: October 10, 2024
Apply Now

A scheme that promotes sustainable agriculture through integrated farming, appropriate soil health management, and resources conservation technology. It aims to make agriculture more productive, sustainable, and climate resilient.

Benefits:
  • Soil health card and management
  • Training on organic farming
  • Climate resilient agricultural practices
Documents Required:
  • Land Records
  • Identity Proof
  • Bank Account Details
Eligibility:
  • All farmers
  • Priority for small and marginal farmers
  • Farmers in climate vulnerable areas
More Details
MKSP (Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana)
Women Farmers Education Active Last Updated: September 22, 2024
Apply Now

A sub-component of the National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM), this scheme aims to empower women in agriculture by making systematic investments to enhance their participation and productivity, and also create sustainable livelihoods for them.

Benefits:
  • Training and capacity building
  • Support for community investment
  • Access to agricultural inputs
Documents Required:
  • Identity Proof
  • Bank Account Details
  • Self Help Group membership details (if applicable)
Eligibility:
  • Women farmers
  • Women in agriculture-related activities
  • Priority to women-headed households
